About CampusLink Polls & Feedback System

CampusLink's Polls & Feedback system is designed to enhance communication and decision-making within your campus community. Our platform bridges the gap between administration and students, creating a transparent and engaging environment for everyone.

🗳️

Interactive Polling

Create and participate in polls on various campus topics. From academic preferences to event planning, get real-time insights from the student community with beautiful visualizations and instant results.

📊

Real-time Results

Watch poll results update instantly as votes come in. Dynamic progress bars and percentage displays make it easy to understand community preferences at a glance.

💬

Comprehensive Feedback

Collect detailed feedback after events, on facilities, or any campus-related topics. Star ratings and detailed comments help identify areas for improvement.

👥

Role-based Access

Students can vote and provide feedback, while administrators have full dashboard access to create polls, analyze results, and manage the entire system.

🔒

Privacy Options

Support for both anonymous and identified responses. Students can choose to provide feedback anonymously, encouraging honest and open communication.

📈

Analytics Dashboard

Comprehensive statistics and insights for administrators. Track participation rates, analyze trends, and make data-driven decisions for campus improvements.
